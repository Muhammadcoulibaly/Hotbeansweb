Requirements for Web development

On this page, you will find information on job specifications as a web developer as well as how to kick-start your application if you want to apply to work for us. Here is a list of requirements and responsibilities as a web developer.

Specifications

Web developers build and maintain websites and web applications. For example, websites Netflix, you tube, google search and millions of more websites. Although their work usually focuses solely on the underlying software and databases (known as the ‘back end’), some web developers work on the interface and visual design (the ‘front end’), There are also developers who combine backend and front-end developers which is called (‘full-stack development’).

In an agency or as a freelancer, a web developer’s job is to create products that meet clients’ needs. The work can be particularly varied with many projects to work on simultaneously and lots of meetings with clients to discuss their requirements and update them on progress. A web developers overall task is to make a website, program and service that is reliable responsive. This is so when people go on that website they know how to navigate the website, which can be accessed over the internet from all over the world. Depending on what role you have in the development of the service that will impact on the job title that you have.
